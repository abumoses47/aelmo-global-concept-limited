About Us

AELMO GLOBAL CONCEPT LIMITED: is a proudly Nigerian, multi-sector company with a mission to deliver quality, innovation, and value across diverse industries. From agricultural processing and manufacturing to construction, exports, and beauty care, we serve both local and international clients with excellence and integrity.

Our operations include rice milling and packaging, paint production, organic shea butter, charcoal exports, and construction services.

Each division operates under the highest standards, with a commitment to customer satisfaction, sustainability, and community development.
